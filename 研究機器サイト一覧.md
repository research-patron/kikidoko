- **NIMS (National Institute for Materials Science)** – **NIMS Open Facility (NOF)**: URL – **www.nims-open-facility.jp** (NIMS Open Facility official site) [nims-open-facility.jp](https://www.nims-open-facility.jp/#:~:text=%E3%83%A1%E3%83%8B%E3%83%A5%E3%83%BC%E9%96%89%E3%81%98%E3%82%8B,%E5%85%B1%E7%94%A8%E8%A8%AD%E5%82%99%E6%A4%9C%E7%B4%A2%20%C2%B7%20%E8%A8%AD%E5%82%99%E7%B4%B9%E4%BB%8B%E5%8B%95%E7%94%BB%20%C2%B7%20%E5%85%B1%E7%94%A8%E8%A8%AD%E5%82%99%E5%88%A9%E7%94%A8%E4%BA%8B%E4%BE%8B). Format: **Interactive web portal** (with menu for “共用設備一覧” and search) [nims-open-facility.jp](https://www.nims-open-facility.jp/#:~:text=%E3%83%A1%E3%83%8B%E3%83%A5%E3%83%BC%E9%96%89%E3%81%98%E3%82%8B,%E5%85%B1%E7%94%A8%E8%A8%AD%E5%82%99%E6%A4%9C%E7%B4%A2%20%C2%B7%20%E8%A8%AD%E5%82%99%E7%B4%B9%E4%BB%8B%E5%8B%95%E7%94%BB%20%C2%B7%20%E5%85%B1%E7%94%A8%E8%A8%AD%E5%82%99%E5%88%A9%E7%94%A8%E4%BA%8B%E4%BE%8B). Crawling may be challenging due to the JS verification [nims-open-facility.jp](https://www.nims-open-facility.jp/#:~:text=URL%3A%20https%3A%2F%2Fwww.nims) – automation might need a headless browser.

## Pagination, Formats & Policy Summary

**Formats:** Most national university pages above are **HTML lists or searchable databases**. A few provide **CSV/Excel** export (Hokkaido’s HURED allows CSV download of search results [gfc.hokudai.ac.jp](https://www.gfc.hokudai.ac.jp/apparatus_list/#:~:text=%E7%AD%86%E9%A0%AD%E8%91%97%E8%80%85%EF%BC%9A%20%E7%99%BA%E8%A1%8C%E5%B9%B4%EF%BC%9A%20%EF%BD%9E%20%E9%9B%91%E8%AA%8C%E5%90%8D%EF%BC%9A%20%E6%A4%9C%E7%B4%A2,%E3%83%AA%E3%82%BB%E3%83%83%E3%83%88%20CSV%E5%87%BA%E5%8A%9B)). Many older sites rely on **PDF files** for equipment catalogs (e.g. Shizuoka Univ., AIST facilities) – those have been noted and excluded as data sources in our list. The newer integrated systems (Tohoku, UTokyo, Osaka, Kyushu, Nagoya, Yamagata, Nagasaki etc.) use **web portals with pagination or dynamic search** interfaces instead of static files. In such cases, scraping might require simulating searches or using provided APIs (if any). For example, UTokyo’s ICS shows page numbers [ics-com.biz](https://www.ics-com.biz/todai_kyouyou/portals/machine/%E3%81%9D%E3%81%AE%E4%BB%96%E3%81%AE%E6%A9%9F%E5%99%A8#:~:text=%E6%9D%B1%E4%BA%AC%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%A6%20%E5%85%B1%E7%94%A8%E6%A9%9F%E5%99%A8%E7%AE%A1%E7%90%86%E3%82%B7%E3%82%B9%E3%83%86%E3%83%A0.%20,151%20%E4%BB%B6%E3%81%8C%E8%A6%8B%E3%81%A4%E3%81%8B%E3%82%8A%E3%81%BE%E3%81%97%E3%81%9F%E3%80%82%E7%A0%94%E7%A9%B6%E8%A8%AD%E5%82%99%E5%90%8D%E7%A7%B0%E3%82%92%E3%82%AF%E3%83%AA%E3%83%83%E3%82%AF%E3%81%99%E3%82%8B%E3%81%A8%E8%A9%B3%E7%B4%B0%E6%83%85%E5%A0%B1) and Osaka’s OPF has filter menus with 0 results until searched [opf.osaka-u.ac.jp](https://www.opf.osaka-u.ac.jp/instruments#:~:text=%E6%A9%9F%E5%99%A8%E5%90%8D%E3%80%81%E3%83%A1%E3%83%BC%E3%82%AB%E3%83%BC%E3%80%81%E5%9E%8B%E7%95%AA%E7%AD%89), indicating the need to query the interface.

**Pagination:** Where applicable, we’ve indicated if the listing spans multiple pages:

- **Pagination present:** UTokyo ICS (pages of ~20 each) [ics-com.biz](https://www.ics-com.biz/todai_kyouyou/portals/machine/%E3%82%A4%E3%83%A1%E3%83%BC%E3%82%B8%E3%83%B3%E3%82%B0%E9%96%A2%E9%80%A3%E6%A9%9F%E5%99%A8#:~:text=50%20%E4%BB%B6%20%E3%81%8C%E8%A6%8B%E3%81%A4%E3%81%8B%E3%82%8A%E3%81%BE%E3%81%97%E3%81%9F%E3%80%82%E7%A0%94%E7%A9%B6%E8%A8%AD%E5%82%99%E5%90%8D%E7%A7%B0%E3%82%92%E3%82%AF%E3%83%AA%E3%83%83%E3%82%AF%E3%81%99%E3%82%8B%E3%81%A8%E8%A9%B3%E7%B4%B0%E6%83%85%E5%A0%B1%E3%81%8C%E8%A1%A8%E7%A4%BA%E3%81%95%E3%82%8C%E3%81%BE%E3%81%99%E3%80%82); Tsukuba OpenFacility (pages of 10/20/50) [openfacility.sec.tsukuba.ac.jp](https://openfacility.sec.tsukuba.ac.jp/public_eq/front.php?cont=eq_index#:~:text=%E4%B8%80%E8%88%AC%E5%90%91%E3%81%91%E5%85%B1%E5%90%8C%E5%88%A9%E7%94%A8%20%E4%B8%80%E8%88%AC%E5%90%91%E3%81%91%E5%A7%94%E8%A8%97%E5%AF%BE%E5%BF%9C); Osaka OPF (dynamic – user selects category then sees pages) [opf.osaka-u.ac.jp](https://www.opf.osaka-u.ac.jp/instruments#:~:text=%E6%A9%9F%E5%99%A8%E3%82%AB%E3%83%86%E3%82%B4%E3%83%AA%E3%83%BC); Kyushu’s ShareAid (unknown number of pages, likely many given number of devices); NIMS NOF (likely dynamic filtering).
- **Single-page listings:** Many universities (Kitami, Muroran, etc.) have all items on one page due to fewer instruments. Also Kyoto’s list is one long page [t.kyoto-u.ac.jp](https://www.t.kyoto-u.ac.jp/ja/research/yui/list#:~:text=), and TMDU’s list is single-page [tmd.ac.jp](https://www.tmd.ac.jp/rcmd/equipment/#:~:text=%E9%81%BA%E4%BC%9D%E5%AD%90%E8%A7%A3%E6%9E%90).
- **PDF catalogs:** AIST’s facility device lists (e.g. NPF “装置一覧” 70KB PDF) [aist.go.jp](https://www.aist.go.jp/aist_j/business/alliance/orp/index.html#:~:text=%E5%85%B7%E7%8F%BE%E5%8C%96%E3%81%99%E3%81%B9%E3%81%8F%E3%80%81%E6%97%A5%E3%80%85%E5%85%88%E7%AB%AF%E7%9A%84%E3%81%AA%E6%9D%90%E6%96%99%E3%83%BB%E3%83%87%E3%83%90%E3%82%A4%E3%82%B9%E3%81%AE%E5%BE%AE%E7%B4%B0%E5%8A%A0%E5%B7%A5%E3%81%8B%E3%82%89%E6%A9%9F%E8%83%BD%E6%A4%9C%E8%A8%BC%E3%81%BE%E3%81%A7%E3%82%92%E4%B8%80%E8%B2%AB%E3%81%97%E3%81%A6%E6%8E%A8%E9%80%B2%E3%81%97%E3%81%A6%E3%81%84%E3%81%BE%E3%81%99%E3%80%82) and some university centers (Shizuoka, Yamanashi etc.) use PDFs – these are not suitable for direct crawling.

**Usage Terms / Robots:** Generally, these pages are meant for public information, so **no robots.txt blocks** were encountered on the main university domains for these paths. However, caution is needed:

- Some systems require user login for reservations or detailed internal info (but **the public lists are accessible without login**). For example, Yamagata’s portal requires account for booking but list is public [yucf2.id.yamagata-u.ac.jp](https://yucf2.id.yamagata-u.ac.jp/eportal#:~:text=%E3%80%8C%E8%A8%AD%E5%82%99%E4%B8%80%E8%A6%A7%E3%80%8D%E3%82%88%E3%82%8A%E6%A4%9C%E7%B4%A2%E3%81%AE%E4%B8%8A%E3%80%81%E5%88%A9%E7%94%A8%E4%BA%88%E7%B4%84%E3%82%92%E8%A1%8C%E3%81%A3%E3%81%A6%E3%81%8F%E3%81%A0%E3%81%95%E3%81%84%E3%80%82), and UTokyo’s ICS allows viewing but login for reservation.
- **Rate limiting** and polite crawling should be observed. The sites are not explicitly API endpoints, so scraping should be gentle to avoid violation of any implicit terms.
- Always check if an equipment page has a “利用規約” (terms of use) link. E.g., AIST has usage regulations linked on its page [aist.go.jp](https://www.aist.go.jp/aist_j/business/alliance/orp/ibec/ja/application.html#:~:text=%E5%85%B1%E7%94%A8%E6%96%BD%E8%A8%AD%E3%81%AE%E3%81%94%E5%88%A9%E7%94%A8%E6%96%B9%E6%B3%95%20) [aist.go.jp](https://www.aist.go.jp/aist_j/researcher/aboutus/howto02.html#:~:text=%E7%94%A3%E7%B7%8F%E7%A0%94%E3%81%AE%E6%96%BD%E8%A8%AD%E3%83%BB%E8%A3%85%E7%BD%AE%E3%82%92%E5%88%A9%E7%94%A8%E3%81%97%E3%81%9F%E3%81%84%20%E3%82%B9%E3%83%BC%E3%83%91%E3%83%BC%E3%82%AF%E3%83%AA%E3%83%BC%E3%83%B3%E3%83%AB%E3%83%BC%E3%83%A0%E3%81%AA%E3%81%A9%E3%82%92%E3%81%AF%E3%81%98%E3%82%81%E3%80%8112%E3%81%AE%E5%85%B1%E7%94%A8%E6%96%BD%E8%A8%AD%E3%81%A7%E3%80%81400%E5%8F%B0%E7%A8%8B%E5%BA%A6%E3%81%AE%E8%A3%85%E7%BD%AE%E3%81%8C%E5%88%A9%E7%94%A8%E5%8F%AF%E8%83%BD%E3%81%A7%E3%81%99%EF%BC%882024%E5%B9%B44%E6%9C%88%E7%8F%BE%E5%9C%A8%EF%BC%89%E3%80%82%20%E7%A0%94%E7%A9%B6%E8%A3%85%E7%BD%AE%E3%81%AE%E5%88%A9%E7%94%A8%E3%81%AB%E3%81%A4%E3%81%84%E3%81%A6), and any such terms (often about contractual usage of equipment) should be respected (though they concern usage agreements rather than web data).
- No explicit “do not crawl” notices were seen. The only partial obstacle is NIMS’s site requiring JS to verify not a robot [nims-open-facility.jp](https://www.nims-open-facility.jp/#:~:text=URL%3A%20https%3A%2F%2Fwww.nims) – a hint that automated scraping needs to mimic a real browser.

In summary, **all national universities with science/engineering programs are covered above with their equipment list URLs**, mostly as HTML pages or portals. The format ranges from simple lists to sophisticated search systems. **Where only PDFs were provided (no HTML listing), we’ve noted that and omitted those as primary data sources.** Researchers implementing the crawler should target the HTML portals and be mindful of pagination and login requirements, and adhere to each site’s usage guidelines and robots policies (e.g., avoid scraping pages that are clearly marked for internal use only).
